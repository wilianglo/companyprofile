PT. Sinar Harapan Baja Master is Distributor Company has been a leader in the Steel distribution industry since its establishment in 2001. As an experienced company, we have built a strong reputation for providing high-quality products and the best services to our customers.

With our commitment to excellence, we have expanded our product range to include steel concrete, plates, angle steel, pipe steel, wiresmesh, CNP, UNP and various other types of steel. We take pride in being a reliable partner for contractors, factories, and construction businesses throughout our region.

Our Vision :

Our vision is to become a nationally and internationally recognized leading steel distributor. We aim to continuously grow and thrive in this industry by prioritizing quality, customer satisfaction, and innovation in every aspect of our business. We dream of building a stronger foundation for the future by meeting the sustainable steel needs worldwide.

Our Mission

  1. Providing High-Quality Products: We are committed to delivering high-quality steel products that meet stringent industry standards (SNI certification), thus helping construction and manufacturing projects become stronger and more durable.
  2. Customer Satisfaction: We believe that our success depends on customer satisfaction. We always listen to the needs of our customers and strive to provide the best solutions that meet their expectations.
  3. Innovation and Continuous Improvement: We continuously seek ways to enhance our business processes and adopt the latest technology to optimize efficiency.
  4. Sustainability and Environmentally Friendly: We are committed to running our operations with care for the environment, including responsible waste management and the selection of environmentally friendly products.
  5. Strong Partnerships: We aim to build enduring and mutually beneficial relationships with suppliers, customers, and our local community.

With this vision and mission, SHBM Steel Distributor Company hopes to continue growing and thriving while remaining the top choice in the iron and steel distribution industry.
